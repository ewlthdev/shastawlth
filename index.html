More Information

  • Transparent: Control and audit your savings at all times. Middlemen can't tamper with funds, or prevent access.

  • Open: Fairly leverages micro-dividends generated from deposits and withdrawals of TRX. All money stays between users!

  • Liquid: Deposit or withdraw easily any time. The smart contract works with any amount.
  • A 10% dividend is paid out proportionally, in TRX, to all tWLTH holders whenever someone buys or sells.

  • The more tWLTH are bought or sold, the more dividends are generated.

  • Every tWLTH bought automatically increases the price of the next, each one sold decreases the price.
